GENERAL CONDITIONS

FIRST.- This authorization implicitly includes the express mandate and necessary powers in favor of Inverstach, SLU to market and manage the property subject to the assignment, and in this capacity, lease it and transfer it to third parties as a tourist rental property, and in general, perform any act of administration that is consistent and necessary for the purpose of the assignment and the purpose of this contract, which is none other than its commercial exploitation using the means and services it offers.

SECOND.- During the specified period, the assignee Inverstach, SLU, will have full and effective use of the property. In the event that during this period, the owner wishes to use the property personally, which is incompatible with its commercial exploitation, it will be necessary to contact Inverstach, SLU in advance to verify if the property is available. In case of sale or transfer to third parties, the transferring owner is obliged to respect the commitments made by Inverstach, SLU. The owner is obliged to make the property available to Inverstach, SLU, 7 days before the arrival of the first client, in order to verify that everything is in order. In any case, this professional assignment is essentially revocable by the owner, but the owner is always obliged to respect the contracts and reservations made by the professional using the conferred powers, and in any case, if the owner does not wish to respect them, as a freely consented penalty clause, they must pay compensation to the professional manager of €2,000.00 euros.

THIRD.- The owner undertakes to carry out the necessary repairs and work to keep the property in perfect conditions of habitability, services, and cleanliness at the beginning of the season for use, including the initial complete cleaning at the beginning of each season. Special attention will be paid to replacing furniture that has deteriorated as a result of continuous use, and efforts will always be made to improve the household equipment of the property. Inverstach, SLU is authorized to remove or replace materials or utensils during the term of this contract due to their deteriorated condition or the owner's failure to replace them, as well as to carry out minor repair and maintenance work, with these expenses being borne by the owner. Any damage to the property caused by the tenant's misuse or negligence will be the tenant's responsibility, and Inverstach, SLU undertakes to make the necessary replacements or repairs and charge the tenant for the cost of the damage.

FOURTH.- The accommodation will be rented furnished and equipped; in the case of having utility services, the costs of water and electricity consumption will be the responsibility of the owner, as well as all expenses and taxes derived from the ownership of the property (Community of Owners, if applicable, property tax, etc.). At the time of contracting, at the beginning of each season, the owner must provide a copy of the mandatory insurance policy for the apartment.

FIFTH.- At the end of the summer season, starting from October 1st, the assignee Inverstach, SLU will settle with the owner the rentals made, based on the prices listed in the specific conditions section of this contract. In case the owner desires an advance payment, this can be done at the owner's request, once the first rental of the season has been received, and the maximum amount to be delivered will be 60% of the total price to be received by the owner.

SIXTH.- After the departure of the last tenant, the owner has fifteen business days to report any damage or issues with the property to Inverstach, SLU; after this period, the owner releases Inverstach, SLU from any liability. Similarly, Inverstach, SLU assumes responsibility for anything that happens within the property after the arrival of the first client and not before.

SEVENTH.- The rentals that are made will always be rentals for tourist use, and consequently, they will be subject to the specific Catalan legislation in this matter. Subsidiarily, the Civil Code will apply, with the Urban Leases Law being expressly excluded. In the event that the owner does not reside in Spain, they declare that they are aware of the tax obligations established in Spanish law resulting from the income of a property under a rental regime. The transferring owner declares under their responsibility that, in the case of properties governed by the system of Community of Owners, it does not contain any rules in its Statutes that prohibit tourist use or commercial exploitation of the apartment. In the event that the Community of Owners amends its Statutes to prohibit tourist activity or commercial exploitation of the apartment, and the order becomes effective, preventing its normal exploitation, the owner will compensate the assignee for all useful expenses incurred in advertising and marketing the property, as well as for claims for damages and losses that tenant clients may present, if applicable.

EIGHTH.- In the event of a reservation cancellation, the non-refunded or withheld compensations from the client's reservation deposit (20%) will be shared 50% between the Agency and the owner, in order to compensate the Agency for advertising and prior arrangements expenses, such as the reservation itself.

NINTH.- In the attached Annex, which is an integral part of this contract, the parties specify the minimum equipment of the property, both in terms of furniture and appliances, and it is also graphically depicted through color photographs.

TENTH.- The lessor states that they are aware of all tax obligations related to the lease of the mentioned property, and in particular, the provisions of Royal Legislative Decree 5/2004 of May 5, which regulates the Income Tax of Non-Residents.
